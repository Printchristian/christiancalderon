When I'm not diving into code or scribbling down my latest idea, you'll probably find me doing the following.

Music

Making music at home is one of my favorite ways to unwind and express myself. I don't need a fancy setup — just my laptop, some headphones, and the right vibe. It's my little space to create something out of nothing.

Sometimes I'll spend hours layering sounds, tweaking beats, or just messing around until something clicks. There's no pressure, no rules — just me and the music. I love getting lost in that process.

writing

I like writing stories, big or small. Some come from real life, others are pure imagination. Either way, I write what feels true.

I see the scene in my mind first — like a movie already playing. It could be a moment, a line, or the whole movie that wants to come to life.

Predictive Sport Analysis

I like doing prop analysis — using stats to make over/under calls on player performance.

I love the feeling of being right after doing the research — like calling Luka Doncic to go over his PRA and watching it hit. It's not just guessing, it's trusting the numbers and your gut.
